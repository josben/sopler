/. Signed-in vs. Non-signed-in users

Sopler will never ask you to register. That's why in case you want Sopler to remember your previous lists and give you more priviledges over your items you need to sign-in with a social network account.
We recommend that you sign-in for a better Sopler experience and management of your lists.

/.How to create a list

  1. Go to sopler.net
  2. If you are not signed-in, enter a username.
  3. Enter the name of the list you want to create... and press Start!

Symbols

Sidepanel symbols
Add an item
If you are not signed-in, enter a name. Then, enter your item and add it to your list.
Information
Find out the owner of the list and if he's a signed-in user (or not). Check out the name of the list and its creation date.
Share
Copy the link or share it via your favourite social network. Print the list or scan the QR Code.
Sync
See the changes your collaborators do on your list or their comments in real-time.

List symbols
Flag
Show that this is an important item. Non-signed-in users can't un-flag the items of a signed-in user.
Lock
Lock an item. Non-signed-in users can't unlock the items of a signed-in user.
Delete
Delete an item. Non-signed-in users can't delete the items of a signed-in user.
Information
When did this item got checked... and by whom?
